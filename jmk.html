About JMK

Janam Munetra Kalagam (JMK) is a youth-oriented organization focused on education, social welfare, community development and leadership.

Our Ideology

Inspired by the leadership values of Vadachennai Chandra and Rajan, JMK believes in public service, development, education and people-first governance.
